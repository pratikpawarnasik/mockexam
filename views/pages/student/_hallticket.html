Navigating to a Question :

  1. To answer a question, do the following:
    1. Click on the question number in the Question Palette to go to that numbered question directly.
    2. Click on Save & Next to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.
    3. Click on Raise a question to save your answer for the current question, to Raise a question, and then go to the next question.
      Caution: Note that your answer for the current question will not be saved, if you navigate to another question directly by clicking on its question number.
  2. You can view all the questions by clicking on the Question Paper button.

Answering a Question :

  1. Procedure for answering a multiple choice type question:
    1. To select your answer, click on the button of one of the options.
    2. To change your chosen answer, click on the button of another option.
    3. To save your answer, you MUST click on the Save & Next
    4. To mark the question for review, click on the Raise a question
      If an answer is selected for a question that is ' Raise a question', that answer will be considered in the evaluation if it is marked as 'Save & Next', at the time of final submission.
  2. To change your answer to a question that has already been answered, first select that question for answering and then follow the procedure for answering that type of question.
  3. Note that questions for which option has been chosen and answers are saved or raise a question will be considered for evaluation.
